What is OPL?

Online Peer Learning (OPL) is a community of students who share their skills online to educate anyone in need of academic help. Students can join us to make academic videos to teach people all around the world about their skills and passions. You can share your code, videos, or other information with your peers. Our objective is to help the community by sharing the knowledge we have with others. With the use of the internet, it will allow us to share our knowledge with an infinite number of people instantly. In all, Online Peer Learning strives to foster a community of determined and passionate students who are willing to learn from each other and teach the world, using the power of the internet and their knowledge.

Why choose OPL?

You may be wondering, why choose OPL over other educational websites such as Khan Academy? One of the main reasons that make OPL unique is that we offer education from students. This is special because our target demographic for our videos are for people just like us, students that are eager to learn. Amidst this pandemic, more teaching is taught online. Students who are less fortunate that do not get adequate education should have another resource at their disposal to learn from, a resource that is very effective at teaching because it is student-made. Peer-to-peer learning reduces the fear of judgement and increases confidence with other learners. With our content, we will be able to closely relate to our audience because we are students just like them, trying to learn more every day.
