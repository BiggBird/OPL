What is OPL?

Online Peer Learning (OPL) is a community of students who share their skills online to educate anyone in need of academic help. Students can join us to make academic videos to teach people all around the world about their skills and passions. You can share your code, videos, or other information with your peers. Our objective is to help the community by sharing the knowledge we have with others. With the use of the internet, it will allow us to share our knowledge with an infinite number of people instantly. In all, Online Peer Learning strives to foster a community of determined students who are willing to learn from each other and teach the world.

Why choose OPL?

Why choose OPL over other educational websites? One of the main reasons that makes OPL unique is that we offer education from other students. Our target demographic for our videos are for people just like us, students that are eager to learn. Amidst this pandemic, more teaching is taught online, with parents now needing to balance their job and kids. There is always something new to learn to keep those students engaged. Everyone should have a resource to use their disposal. At different ages, everyone learns differently. You can relate to the subject better if it is taught by someone your age because they have both gone through the same difficulties. In addition, peer-to-peer learning reduces the fear of judgement and increases confidence with other learners. We will be able to closely relate to our audience because we are students just like them, trying to learn more every day.
